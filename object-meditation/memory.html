DESIGNED BY KARL WU, SPRING 2020

The story between the pen and me started in Junior school.

My mom gave it to me as a present and encouraged me to practise writing.

Since then I have spent a lot of time with it to enhance my skill.

At first I focused on Chinese calligraphy, then I also practised English.

To me, it is not the skills I learned that made it important to me.

It is those memories of hardwork and sweet moments with my friends that made it special.

These memories are like the fading text. It is a one way trip.
